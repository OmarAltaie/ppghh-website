Our Partnerships

Plymouth Psych Group: Our partnership with Plymouth Psych Group is based on expanding research opportunities in mental health, specifically focusing on Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) treatments and its implications for different psychiatric conditions and patient populations. University of Minnesota School of Nursing: Our partnership with University of Minnesota School of Nursing focuses on creating training opportunities for future Nurse Practitioners who are currently enrolled in University of Minnesota programs. Augsburg University: Our partnership with Augsburg University is aimed to create internship opportunities for students in the Physician Assistant programs.
