PPG Health and Healing Fund (PPGHH) is a non-profit organization established in 2023. The organization was founded to increase access to mental health services to underprivileged communities, expand the current scope of research in the field of mental health and provide educational opportunities for future mental health professionals. PPGHH aims to contribute to the ongoing movement of increasing equity in the field of mental health. With current and future partnerships with mental health and educational organizations, PPGHH strives to become a leading voice in matters of equity in mental health.

TMS Support Group

At Health and Healing, we understand that undergoing Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) treatment can be a transformative journey, both for patients and their families. That's why we offer a comprehensive support group dedicated to providing guidance and encouragement every step of the way. From initial consultations to post-treatment follow-ups, our support network offers a safe space for patients and their loved ones to share experiences, ask questions, and receive emotional support. Furthermore, we foster an environment where patients feel comfortable sharing directly with each other, providing a platform for mutual advice and support. Our compassionate team is here to address concerns, offer resources, and celebrate milestones, ensuring that individuals feel empowered and supported throughout their TMS journey. Join our community and let us walk alongside you as you embark on the path to improved mental wellness.
